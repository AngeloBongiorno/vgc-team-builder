# Pokémon Squad Builder

*! WORK IN PROGRESS !*

A Pokémon VGC team Builder.

This AI-based assistant will help you build a viable Pokémon VGC team.
Integrated with damage calculator, regulation rules check, type chart calculator and web search.

## Functions

- Iteratively ask the assistant to improve your team based on your needs or highlighted weaknesses.
- Access tools such as:
    - Damage calculator
    - Weaknesses/resists calculator
- Have your team sheet visible at all times
- Export your team to PokéPaste
- Future work: get insights from recent tournament results
